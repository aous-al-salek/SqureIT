Data Online

Data is the information you put online, such as photos, videos, or posts. It's important to be careful with what you share, who you share it with, and what websites you use. Always think before you post, and keep your personal information safe.

Think before you post

Everything you post online, whether it's a post, picture, or comment, can be there forever. Even if you delete it, it might still be saved somewhere. So, it's important to think before you post and make sure you're comfortable with it being online forever.

The Internet never forgets but

If you post something online that you shouldn't, delete it as soon as possible. If you don't know how, ask an adult for help.
